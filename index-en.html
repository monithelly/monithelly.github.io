GitHub · Business Case

Distrimax

The Paradox

"How can a company have excess inventory and lost sales at the same time?"

This case simulates a real business problem: overstock coexisting with lost sales. The analysis was structured to identify root causes, quantify financial impact and propose a data-driven solution. More than a dashboard, it is a full diagnostic with strategic direction.

$194M
Uncaptured revenue potential identified
78%
Recoverable with a revised replenishment policy
1,363d
Excess stock coverage in low-priority SKUs
↓23%
Projected reduction in tied-up capital
SQL Window Functions Power BI ABC Analysis Ishikawa GUT Matrix
View on GitHub →
1
Data Modeling
Fictional dataset with real-world structure: 4 tables and 15,900 weekly inventory records by SKU and region.
2
SQL Analysis
4 documented queries: ABC curve, stockout ranking, lost revenue and comparison against the ideal replenishment policy. Used window functions, CASE WHEN and aggregations.
3
Diagnosis
Ishikawa for root cause and GUT Matrix for prioritization. Class A items had an average of 10 days of coverage against a target of 30 to 45 days.
4
Power BI Dashboard
Two pages: problem diagnosis and impact projection under the new ABC-based replenishment policy.
5
Solution and Impact
Differentiated replenishment policy by class A, B and C with review frequency, safety stock and projected financial impact.
